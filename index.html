Welcome to Frogling, a free android apllication, which allows you to share your thoughts through a simple picture with couple fo words. Basically, what you do is just find yourself in an interesting situation, enter the app, and post a frogling (a picture with words) about it. After all, one picture speaks a thousand words, so imagine how much froglings can say!

Features

  • Helps you navigate the web without touching the mouse.
  • Uses a clever highlighting method to navigate using links.
  • Customizable keyboard shortcuts
  • Has an in-page help dialog to remind you of your personalized shortcuts.
